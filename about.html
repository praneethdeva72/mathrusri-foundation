About Us

Learn who we are and what drives us to serve the community.

Our Story

Mathrusri Foundation was founded in 2017 with a simple belief: compassion has the power to transform lives. We work tirelessly to bridge gaps in education, healthcare, and basic needs — reaching the most deserving individuals in our society, right here in Vijayawada and beyond.

Our vision

Creating empowered communities where every individual has access to basic necessities and the opportunity to thrive with dignity.

Our mission

To provide consistent educational, medical, and welfare support to underprivileged sections of society through ethical and transparent means.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make.

Compassion

We lead with empathy, ensuring every individual is treated with care and respect.

Transparency

We maintain open, honest practices in all our operations and use of donations.

Sustainability

We build programs designed to create lasting positive change, not quick fixes.
